NYC 2014 Taxi Pickups Visualization

About


This site takes the advantage of 2014 yellow taxi data from 01/01/2014 to 12/31/2014 from NYC Taxi Limousine Commission and provides a visualization of taxi pickups using Leaflet, CartoDB, Bootstrap, and dc.js. The site is created for the final project of the course Big Data Management and Analysis at Center for Urban Science and Progress.
